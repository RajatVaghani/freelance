Introduction

This article will guide you step by step from nothing to a wordpress website in less than 15 minutes. Whetever you are making a new website for your local business, online store or starting a new blog? This is where you begin.

The first step when creating a website is to buy a domain name. A domain name is what people search for in the browser to get to your website, for example: mydomain.com. Most of the "good" domain names are taken, but if you are creative you will find something for you.
